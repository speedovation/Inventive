HTML Markup for these tabs

\n

The HTML markup for your tabs and content can be arranged however you want. At the minimum, you need a container, a collection of links for your tabs (an unordered list by default), and matching divs for your tabbed content. Make sure the tabhrefattributes match theidof the target panel. This is standard semantic markup for in-page anchors.

The class names above are just to make it easy to style. You can make them whatever you want, there's no magic here.
